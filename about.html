Hi, I'm Vincenzo Nuzzolese, an Italian independent photographer based in Rome, with roots in the city of Matera.

My passion lies in documenting the world through my lens, with a particular focus on politics, social issues, and current affairs. I strive to capture moments of collective significance, telling stories that resonate and spark conversation.

I collaborate with leading photo agencies like SOPA Images and ZUMA Press, and my work has been featured in some of the most respected media outlets worldwide, including The Economist, The Guardian, Les Echos, The Wall Street Journal, BBC, Der Spiegel, Neue Zürcher Zeitung, Internazionale, la Repubblica, Vanity Fair, Huffington Post, Politico, Corriere della Sera, Der Standard, The Post, IndieWire, The Sun, Rai, and L'Espresso.

For information, inquiries, assignments, or funny jokes, feel free to reach out to me at info@vincenzonuzzolese.com
